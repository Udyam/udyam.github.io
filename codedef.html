Introduction

"Talk is cheap.Show me the code"
Do you have the capability to guide your machine to work with 9.8m/s 2 ? If yes,then get ready to demonstrate your algorithmic skills in Udyam'16. Code-Def brings to you the opportunity to put your programming skills to test and see whether your 0's and 1's are wise enough to bring the glory to you. Code-Def spans events ranging from online coding competition as a pre- event and then an on spot coding competition in the main event So, gear up and get ready to release the clutch!!

Problem Statement:

Knowledge of basic programming is needed in every field (every branch). The competition will help participants test as well as groom their skills. We will try to keep questions of all difficulty levels encouraging participation from all level of programmers.
Learning resources will be sent to interested participants.

Event Structure:

The event will be in 2 rounds.
First there will be an online qualification round.
Top participants from this round will be invited for the onsite round during UDYAM-2016.
